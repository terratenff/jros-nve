Here you can interact with various stories, or create one of your own.

%P%

There are three options that you can take from here.

%P%

Option 1 - View a completed story

%P%

Once a reader has filled the prompts for a story, it becomes a complete
story, which can be read by other readers.

%P%

Option 2 - Fill prompts for a story

%P%

Take on a story template by filling the prompts, creating a potentially
interesting story which can then be shared for other readers.

%P%

Option 3 - Create a new story

%P%

Create a new story template for other readers to complete.
